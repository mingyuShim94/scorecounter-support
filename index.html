Frequently Asked Questions

What is Battle Mode?

Battle Mode shows two large score panels side by side — perfect for head-to-head games like tennis, table tennis, basketball, or darts. Tap either panel to add a point. Long press to enter a score directly.

What is Group Mode?

Group Mode lets you track scores for 2 to 8 players in a card list. Each player gets + and − buttons. Swipe left on a player card to remove them from the game.

How do I undo a score change?

Shake your device to undo the last score change. Score Counter supports unlimited undo, so you can shake multiple times to step back through your history.

How do I reset all scores?

In Group Mode, tap the reset icon (↺) in the top right corner. A confirmation dialog will appear before any scores are cleared. In Battle Mode, tap the reset button in the app bar.

How do I rename a team or player?

In Battle Mode, tap the team name label to edit it. In Group Mode, tap the player's name card to rename them.

Can I enter a score directly instead of tapping + repeatedly?

Yes. Long press on any score number to open a direct input dialog where you can type any value.

Does Score Counter save my scores if I close the app?

Score Counter stores your game session locally on your device. Your scores are preserved when you switch apps, but starting a new game session will reset the scores.

Is Score Counter really free with no ads?

Yes. Score Counter is completely free with no advertisements, no in-app purchases, and no account required. Every feature is available from the first tap.

Does Score Counter work offline?

Yes. Score Counter works entirely offline. No internet connection is required and no data is ever sent to any server.
