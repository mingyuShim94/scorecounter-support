Privacy Policy

Score Counter — Keep Score for Any Game
Last updated: February 19, 2026

Score Counter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Overview

Score Counter ("the App") is developed by gguggulab. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

Data Collection

Score Counter does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • No usage analytics or tracking data is collected
  • No game scores or session data are uploaded to any server
  • No cookies or identifiers are used
  • No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included

How the App Works

All score tracking is performed entirely on your device. The App:

  • Stores game sessions and settings locally using on-device storage
  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not require any account or sign-in
  • Does not display any advertisements

Data Storage

Game sessions and app settings (theme, haptic preferences) are stored locally on your device within the App's sandbox. This data is automatically removed when you delete the App.

Third-Party Services

Score Counter does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any user, including children under 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
